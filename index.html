📋 SSDI (Social Security Disability)
If you qualify for SSDI, you receive benefits based on your earnings record. After 24 months on SSDI, you qualify for Medicare (regardless of age). At your Full Retirement Age, SSDI automatically converts to regular retirement benefits at the same amount — there is no early-claiming reduction. Your earnings record is frozen at disability onset, so your PIA is based on earnings up to that point.
